Tranmere Rovers Football Club

Who Are Tranmere Rovers?

Tranmere Rovers Football Club is a professional football team based in Birkenhead, England. They currently play in the EFL League Two, which is the fourth tier of English football. Known as "The Super White Army," Tranmere has a dedicated local fanbase and a rich football history.

Where Are They From?

Tranmere Rovers are based in Birkenhead, a town on the Wirral Peninsula in Merseyside, just across the River Mersey from Liverpool. Their home ground is Prenton Park, which has a capacity of over 16,500.

A Brief History of Tranmere Rovers

Founded in 1884, Tranmere Rovers have experienced ups and downs over the decades. They've had success in the lower leagues and have occasionally come close to breaking into the top tier. In the 1990s, under manager John King, they reached the playoffs for a chance to join the Premier League but narrowly missed out. The club remains a staple of English football with a proud tradition.
